Your Commitment

What We Ask of You

Write your story honestly, and be willing to help market and promote the book.

01
Write Your Chapter

Pen a compelling, transparent narrative chapter (approx. 2,000–3,000 words) detailing your specific publishing journey — the real story, including the structural hurdles and industry triumphs.

02
Collaborative Editing

Engage in a collaborative developmental editing process to ensure a high-quality, polished narrative that serves the broader reader. You retain your voice — we just sharpen it together.

03
Marketing Appearances

Show up for pre-booked marketing appearances further down the road (e.g., guesting on podcasts or participating in virtual workshops). Post to your socials and like the other posts from your co-authors. We handle all scheduling and logistics — you just show up, share your expertise and support your fellow authors.

Representation matters.

To make this a definitive guide, we are seeking a wide spread of genres and author demographics. We recognize that some backgrounds face a much steeper climb in this industry than others. Highlighting those systemic barriers — and how you overcame them — is a core mission of this book. If your journey was hard-won, that is exactly the story we want to tell.
